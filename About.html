What we do

WvW raiding guild

Almost Harmless [AH] is a GW2 guild with the PvP/RvR experience of DaoC, Warhammer and Aion. Founded on Desolation we ventured into Piken to finaly settle on SFR. On our way we kept improving and pushing our abilities to their limits while aiming to maintain/polish a 25man core Raid.

We never back down from a fight   —Tiru

Guild raids are on Sunday, Tuesday and Wednesday. Raids start at 7pm (gmt) except on Fridays when the raid begins at matchup reset (we run open & closed raids).
Monday and Thursday are reserved for scrims and GvGs.
