Who we're looking for

Dedicated, social players willing to learn.

Our rules and regulations are the same you will find in any guild.

  • Be active and understand the game
  • Have the will to improve and learn from your mistakes
  • Be open for alot of theorycrafting, changing sets/builds
  • Work for your server and your guild
  • 100% rep (except personal vault)
  • No Drama

Currently we have several classes in high demand. These include Thief, Necro, Mesmer and Warrior.
If you main any of these classes or have a good understanding of their playstyle feel free to apply.

New members will spend their first few weeks as trial rank to monitor raid attendance and skill level.
